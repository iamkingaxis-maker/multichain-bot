# Explicit EV Model — Solana Dip-Buy Fleet (2026-07-06)

**Method.** Reconstructed position-level round trips: grouped every buy + all its sell legs by
`(bot_id, address, entry_price)`; blended realized pnl = Σ(sell_fraction·pnl_pct)/Σsell_fraction.
2124 fully-closed positions from 5000 legs. EV = p·W − (1−p)·L (pp of position return).
Paper cohorts use decision-price pnl (friction-free) → friction subtracted separately.
LIVE probe pnl is real fills → friction already inside it.

## Verdict

**Not net-positive today.** After real round-trip friction (~3.4pp), every cohort is negative: young lane
−1.6pp, flush −4.3pp, live probe −0.4pp (n=6). Gross paper EV of the young lane (+1.8pp) is real but is
carried entirely by 1–2 tail runners — strip them and the median trade loses money. To cross zero the
young lane must move **from 35.3% to 42.3% win rate at the current 2.62:1 payoff (a +7pp lift)**, OR keep
its win rate and **push the payoff ratio to ≥4:1** (e.g. peel raising avg_win ~30% *and* a tighter stop
cutting avg_loss ~20% drops the breakeven to 31.6%, which today's win rate already beats). The flush
family is ~25pp of win-rate away from breakeven and should not go live at its current payoff. The
HL-confirm mirror has zero CONFIRMED fills, so its effect on p is still unmeasured — that is the first
lever to prove, since TRACKING entries currently underperform even the legacy cohort.
